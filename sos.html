Signs of Solastalgia

11/11-11/22/24

"As you walk through our exhibit, you will witness divergent mediums
and subject matters. Some artworks you physically, visually, or auditorily
absorb. Many, if not all, of the pieces suggest a lingering feeling of
Solastalgia: the emotional distress and hopelessness felt when considering
environmental change. As twelve young adults facing the ever-growing
effects of climate change, solastalgia is a familiar emotional state that we
choose to center and confront in our exhibit; we invite you all to seek out
the artists to ask any further questions on the specifics of their work."
- reception speech
