paper factory

4/25-4/28/25

Paper is just a flat sheet of… paper, there is no need to notice it more,
or to make it interesting but I couldn’t help but want to. With
paper the homogeny is expected, so with this project I wanted each
pieces' queerness to be the highlight. Large sculptures made from
plant fiber, thick books embedded with trash, and me the person
who made it all. To understand the complexities you can't augle
from afar, you have to interact.
